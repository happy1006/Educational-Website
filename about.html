Our Objective :-

Since, education is for all and so is our objective. This website is an open-source and free for all. So, whether a student is poor or rich, this platform will treat all its students in the same manner. All the students are same for us. Here, we provide live doubt classes so that students who are facing difficulty in any topic can chat with the teacher available and clear their respective doubts. Those who want to contribute in it in any possible way are heartly welcome and their contribution will be really appreciated. At last, student satisfaction is our first priority.....
